Welcome to Newcastle Table Tennis

Looking for a place to play table tennis in Newcastle upon Tyne? You've come to the right place! Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, Newcastle has plenty of opportunities for you to enjoy this exciting sport.

Where to Play

  • Newcastle Table Tennis Club: Located in the heart of the city, this club offers top-notch facilities and coaching for all skill levels. Perfect for both casual play and competitive matches.
  • Northumberland Table Tennis Club: A friendly club with a welcoming atmosphere, ideal for those who want to improve their skills and meet fellow table tennis enthusiasts.
  • Local Leisure Centres: Many leisure centres in Newcastle have table tennis tables available for public use. Check out your nearest centre for availability and booking options.
